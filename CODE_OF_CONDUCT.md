# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

napari-vipp should be a welcoming, rigorous, and constructive project for
people with different scientific, technical, professional, and personal
backgrounds.

Expected behavior includes:

- treating other participants with respect and empathy;
- discussing ideas and evidence without personal attacks;
- giving specific, actionable, and proportionate feedback;
- acknowledging uncertainty, mistakes, and the work of others;
- respecting privacy, consent, authorship, and research-data restrictions; and
- prioritizing the safety and accessibility of the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, intimidation,
sexualized conduct, deliberate misgendering, doxxing, threats, sustained
disruption, plagiarism, retaliation, or sharing private or restricted material
without permission.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews,
documentation, project events, and other spaces where someone represents the
project.

## Reporting And Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer
privately through the contact details on the
[maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/rensutheart). Do not include
sensitive details in a public issue. Reports will be handled as confidentially
as practical, with conflicts of interest considered when choosing who reviews a
report.

Responses may include a private correction, warning, content removal,
temporary restriction, or permanent exclusion, depending on the severity and
pattern of behavior. Deliberately false or retaliatory reports are themselves a
violation; a report made in good faith is not.

Project maintainers are responsible for applying this policy consistently and
may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate it.
